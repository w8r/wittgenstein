5.2522
The general term of the formal series a, O' a, O' O' a, . . . I write thus: "[a, x, O' x]". This expression in brackets is a variable. The first term of the expression is the beginning of the formal series, the second the form of an arbitrary term x of the series, and the third the form of that term of the series which immediately follws x.
