5.5261
A completely generalized proposition is like every other proposition composite. (This is shown by the fact that in "(x, ) . x" we must mention "" and "x" separately. Both stand independently in signifying relations to the worled as in the ungeneralized proposition.)

A characteristic of a compositie symbol: it has something in common with other symbols.
