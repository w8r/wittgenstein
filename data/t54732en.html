5.4732
We cannot give a sign the wrong sense.

5.47321
Occam's razor is, of course, not an arbitrary rule nor one justified by its practical success. It simply sais that unnecessary elements in a symbolism mean nothing.

Signs which serve one purpose are logically equivalent, signs which serve no purpose are logically meaningless.
