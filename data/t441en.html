4.41
The truth-possibilities of the elementary propositions are the conditions of the truth and falsehood of the propositions.

4.411
It seems probable even at first sight that the introduction of the elementary propositions is fundamental for the comprehension of the other kinds of propositions. Indeed the comprehension of the general propositions depends palpably on that of the general propositions.
